Kerry Group Sports Day

Kerry Logistics Thailand has held a Sports Day for all Kerry business units on 21 November 2015 at Sriracha Municipality Stadium at Chonburi.  More than 100 participants from Kerry Express, led by K. Alex Ng (Director and General Manager), K. Michael Choi (Deputy General Manager) and K. Jakpecth Ratcharoensook (Assistant General Manager), participated in matches of basketball, volleyball, football, badminton and trakor.
This Sports Day proved that we, Kerry Express, are a very strong team.   We were the champion of the day and we have won:

1.      Champion of Sepaktrakraw (Men)
2.      Champion of Basketball (Men)
3.      Champion of Basketball (Women)
4.      Champion of Badminton doubles (Men)
5.      Champion of Volleyball (Men)
6.      Champion of Sack race
7.      Champion of Parades and Cheering
