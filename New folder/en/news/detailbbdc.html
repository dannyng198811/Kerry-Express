KETH has participated in an exhibition “Last Mile Fulfilment Asia (LMFAsia) 2016” at Singapore Expo

After 3-month’ preparation, KETH has participated in an exhibition “Last Mile Fulfilment Asia (LMFAsia) 2016” at Singapore Expo on 03-04 Mar 2016.  Being the Gold Sponsor of the event, Kerry Express had set up the booth mimicking a parcel shop with a motorbike of Bangkok Sameday sent from Bangkok to the venue for display.  A strong team comprising of Kerry representative from Thailand, Hong Kong, Taiwan, Vietnam and Malaysia attended to answer visitors’ enquiries and explore business opportunities with other industry players.

Mr. Alex Ng (Director and General Manager, KETH), as one of the panelists of LMFAsia 2016 International Advisory Panel, was invited to share how KETH works with other private enterprises to drive the e-commerce industry together in a seminar “Initiatives to Support the Growth of Cross-Border Ecommerce, Transportation and Transit” along with other e-commerce and 3PL expertise.
          
LMFAsia is the only Asian conference and exhibition dedicated to eCommerce fulfilment.  Themed “eCommerce Beyond Borders”, the event featured over 90 conference speakers and over 90 exhibitors from Asia.
