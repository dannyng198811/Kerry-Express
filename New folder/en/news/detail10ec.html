Kerry Express’ service counters in the Rabbit Service Point of Bangkok’s BTS stations

Kerry Express is the best domestic parcel delivery platform in Thailand, in terms of geographic coverage and level of services. Its next-day (ND) delivery service covers 99% of all postal codes; and 98% of the parcels are delivered within the estimated delivery time. Kerry Express serves thousands of businesses, individuals and households in sending its parcels to anywhere you can imagine in Thailand.
 
Kerry Express first stepped into the C2C (individual-to-individual) parcel service in September 2013, when the first Parcel Shop was opened in Asoke Tower.  The Parcel Shop quickly became successful and popular in the community:  hundreds of users start to enjoy the quick, simple, reliable and stylish service that Kerry Express provides to the community.   New Shops expanded quickly to Siam Square, Silom, Union Mall, Bangyai Market, Rangsit University, Kingkeaw, Mahachai, Pakkred, Muangthong Thani, Seacon, Bangkae, Bangbuathong, and Happyland in 2014.
 
“Anywhere in Thailand, Next-day Delivery, Service Guaranteed” is the motto of Kerry Express Parcel Shop.   The company changed the long traditions of complicate, untraceable and invisible public postal service into a simple, fast, predictable and, most importantly, affordable nationwide courier service. With the presence of Rabbit Service Points on eight BTS stations, Kerry Express has taken the opportunity to start its station-to-station (same day) and station-to-home (next day) parcel services for customers visiting the Service Points.
